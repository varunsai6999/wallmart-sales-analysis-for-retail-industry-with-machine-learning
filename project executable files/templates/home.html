Walmart Sales Analysis

Welcome to the Walmart Sales Analysis project! This tool is designed to help you predict future sales based on various factors such as store number, department number, size, temperature, CPI, unemployment rate, holiday status, weekend status, and more.

By leveraging a trained Random Forest model, this application provides accurate sales predictions that can help in making informed business decisions. Simply fill out the required fields in the prediction form and get instant sales forecasts.

This project is part of an effort to utilize data science and machine learning techniques to enhance retail operations and provide actionable insights.
